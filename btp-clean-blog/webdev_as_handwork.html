WEB DEVELOPMENT AS AN HANDWORK

You will love it once you take a step to start

Posted by Start Bootstrap on August 24, 2018

You having the knowledge of web development is not just to design web on your own it is also as good as having a handiwork.

As we all know the common handwork in Nigeria are tailoring, hairdressing, mechanic, barber etc. a whole lot of people are already into this and it is choked up that there is no more room to make tangible money due to huge competition as you will see nothing less than 3 – 4 hairdressers, barbers or tailors in one street of Lagos especially.

No wonder a lot of these people won’t cease complaining, languishing in financial crisis. Same is going on with a lot out there they want to learn handwork and they don’t know the right path to follow all because they never knew or could it be ignorance?

As a web developer you can’t be broke as long as you are creative your check keeps rolling in every day.

SOURCE OF INCOME

Last year I made close to $50,000 dollars I am still on loose for more this year.

I got income from my sites, yes siteS NOT site there are lot ways and methods of making legitimate money online with your website (that is if you have any) one of them is freelancing - last week i got over $80 dollars through fiverr from 5 clients the most thrilling part is that I still earn from the part-time jobs I do! (I love money you'll say My fault?) you see I use just one stone to kill a lot of birds NO! Money (I am not getting birds in my account).

Have family members who are going to get you gifts or advice you to learn handwork? Ask them to invest in a proper website design course for you.

To be sincere, if I had learn tailoring or barbing or hairdressing I wouldn’t have gotten this much in a short time and still have time for myself. Most of my friends are now coming back to me to learn web development.

INCREASE YOUR POTENTIAL TARGET MARKET

While it may not be the goal of every small or medium size business to go international, a website can increase exposure to more people inside and outside of your current area of focus.

That small shop you are cannot boom your business do not expect turnover overnight because it cannot increase your turnover you need to go out of the box to make enormous sales.
Aren’t you worried that your turnover rate is slow? Simply because your business is only within you locality, if you want to see your checks rolling in then you have to go beyond your locality.

What we mean is that you have to publicize your business to other various places, community, society, Locality even states.

So how do you do increase sales? What is the easiest way of achieving this?

The easiest and best way is through networking.
The main advantage of networking is that you can share unlimited information and resources getting vast audience, having access to, and being at different places at the same time.

Now how do you network your business?

It is very simple in this 21st century if your business is not online you are seriously lagging.
For you to network your business so as to get to wide range of people turning prospect to customer – customer to sales – sales to increase turnover for your business, you have to put it online! and that is why you need a website to promote your business.

INCREASED CONVENIENCE FOR A CUSTOMER

Whether you are selling products or services, a website is a convenient way for a potential client or current customer to purchase products, get information, contact you or your office and give feedback. Great advantage of having a website is that you can list the web address on any piece of advertising, and a potential client can go to your site and get plenty of information.

PROFESSIONALISM

The amount of users on the web has grown 200% each year over the past 3 years. If you look at Fortune 500 company, then you will notice that almost all of them have a website. A well-done website, including your own domain name (www.yourcompanyname(dot)com), communicates to a potential client that your company is professional and is doing well.

Websites are a relatively inexpensive means of advertising.
